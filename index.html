Our Services

Mechanical Services

We carry out general maintenance, preventive maintenance, installation of mechanical assets, attending to reactive complaints & troubleshooting.

Electrical Services

We maintain distribution boards, control panels and low voltage (LV) panels, capacitor banks as well as lighting systems going through all preventative electrical maintenance, providing all sorts of professionals including Welders, steel fixer.etc.

Plumbing Services

We can adapt to suit the needs of our clients, including upgrades and repairs, as well as installations. We are the first port of call for the plumbing needs of our clients, delivering an exceptional standard of service.

HVAC

We provide a wide range of services, including the management of fresh air handling units (FAHU), Air handling units (AHU), Fan cooling units (FCU) and chillers and heat exchangers. Our HVAC services are available to both residential and commer cial properties.

Housekeeping Services

With advanced experience and knowledge in deep cleaning residential and commercial spaces, we will work with you to deliver to your expectations. If you need our cleaning services regularly, we can adapt to suit your individual needs. The positions we provide include catering helpers, packing helpers.etc.

Premise Deep Cleaning

Our cleaning staff will be happy to provide a cleaning service, both big or small, commercial or residential. We understand the importance of delivering a vality cleaning service, and we can also respond to immediate adhoc cleaning requirements or ongoing contractual ones.

Security Services

We offer compentent security guards which secures premises and personnel by patrolling property, monitoring surveillance equipment, and access points. Investigates security breaches, incidents, and other alarming behavior. Controls traffic by directing drivers. Completes reports by recording observations, information, occurrences, and surveillance activities.

Building and Turnkey Projects

We can provide complete fitouts and refur bishments, all types of flooring work bathroom fitouts, kitchen fitouts, carpentry and joinery, decorative and simple ceiling works, blockwork, tiling, painting and more. The professionals provided by us include carpenters, Mesons, Tiler, Wall painters.etc.

Manpower Supply

We at KEMOS are prepared to provide staff to work on your projects our team capacity caters to small to large sized projects. We have highly experienced and trained staff available to cover a range of roles.
